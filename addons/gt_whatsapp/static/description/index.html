Overview

Odoo Whatsapp connector and integration developed by Globalteckz allow you to share messages from Odoo ERP software to your Whatsapp contacts. You can set Whatsapp numbers of your clients, vendors, suppliers and Odoo and share messages from Odoo erp software. This module help you to communicate at ease with your vendors and customers. This module is further customizable as per your business requirements.

Features

Quick installation

Send Whatsapp message directly from Odoo

Whats up to your customers

Create order confirmation whats up messages to clients

Send invoice paid confirmation

Fully Customizable
