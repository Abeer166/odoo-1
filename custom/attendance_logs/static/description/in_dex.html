EMPLOYEE'S DAILY ATTENDANCE TRACKING

Overview

Employee's Daily Attendance will be tracked by Office Planned Hours along withEmployee Work Hours based on that system will easily track Late Arrivals & Early Go by day-to-day by each employee.

Key Features

Employee Form -- Working Hours will be configured as per office official schedule against each employee.

Employee's Attendance Page will display Office Planned Hours Employee Work Hours ,Late Arrivals, Early Go

Employee's Attendance Month wise Reports Graph View
